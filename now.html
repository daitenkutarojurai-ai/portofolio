What I'm working on right now.

A snapshot of the current season — what's on the bench, what's printing overnight, what's shipping next. This is a /now page in the Derek-Sivers tradition. Updated roughly monthly.

Last updated · April 2026
On the bench

Cert Quest — Play Store closed test → public launch

Wrapping the Play Store closed-test cycle for Cert Quest. Question packs across multiple IT-cert vendors are stable; the gamification layer (XP, streak, daily challenge) is the last thing being tuned before public release.

Next vendor on the roadmap: Microsoft Azure (AZ-900 then AZ-104).

Printing

Chess set #6 — silhouette study

Designing a sixth original chess set in Fusion 360. The constraint this time: each piece readable from across the table at one glance, like the four-player Chaturanga — but for two players.

Print queue: 6 pieces × ~5h each on the Bambu, PLA matte black + bone white.

Shipping

Glaces en Seine — engine v3, sell-through log

Glaces en Seine v3 will accept a nightly POST of {date, crepes, glaces, waffles} sales and train a lightweight regression on briefing signals vs. real sales — so the Sunday forecast actually learns from past Sundays.

Also adding a shopping-list generator scaled by the recipe-scaler batter percentage.

The HashiCorp Terraform Bootcamp season wrapped. The next season is likely HashiCorp Vault (more demand from the audience) or Packer (shorter, gets me back to baking faster). Polling on Instagram @diyfunproject.

Not doing

What I deliberately said no to this season

The corollary to a good now page is the list of things you said no to. This season, I'm not:

  • Starting a YouTube channel — write-ups on Hackster & Instructables already cover the same ground for me, and video editing eats workshop time.
  • Adding a Discord — the existing channels (Instagram DMs, GitHub issues, email) already cover what I can answer in a week.
  • Rewriting older Hackster posts — the audience has moved on; new builds compound better than refurbishing old ones.

If you came here looking for any of those — sorry, won't happen this season. Maybe later.
