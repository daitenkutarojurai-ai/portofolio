About

Ten years of making things in public.

I'm Thomas — a maker based between Luxembourg and rural France. I've been publishing open hardware and apps since 2016, mostly on Hackster and Instructables, more recently on GitHub and the Play Store.

The through-line: take a familiar object, give it a brain, put it online, write down how I did it. Cumulatively those write-ups have pulled in 100,000+ views and a small, loyal audience of fellow tinkerers.

Off the keyboard I run marathons, play chess, design STL models, and host a handful of small podcasts.
