03 — Profile About

Ten years of making things in public.

The work on this site started as a teenage soldering habit and never really stopped.

“I like the moment a project stops being a sketch and starts being a thing in the room.” — Thomas Fendrich

I'm Thomas — a maker and builder based between Luxembourg and rural France. I've been publishing open hardware and apps since 2016, mostly on Hackster and Instructables, more recently on GitHub and the Play Store.

The through-line across everything here: take a familiar object, give it a brain, put it online, and write down how I did it so the next person can skip the frustrating bits. Cumulatively, those write-ups have pulled in 100,000+ views and a small, loyal audience of fellow tinkerers.

Off the keyboard I run marathons, play a lot of chess, design STL models for 3D printing, and host a handful of small podcasts. I draft better ideas than I ship — but I try to fix that by finishing at least one silly thing per quarter.
