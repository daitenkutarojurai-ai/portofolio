2026 · 04 · 11

Glaces en Seine — engine v2 anchored on the upcoming Sunday

SoftwareGlaces en SeineNext.js

Reworked the briefing engine so every metric (cardigan threshold, thermal pivot, bribe-o-meter, ferry pulses) targets the upcoming Sunday rather than "today". Open-Meteo is queried with start_date=end_date= for the exact target date. The board rolls forward at Monday 00:00 local.

The dashboard now feels like an actual briefing instead of a snapshot — you load it on Wednesday and it's already telling you what Sunday looks like.
